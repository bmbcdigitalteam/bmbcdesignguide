Inaccessible document email template

For documents that aren't accessible

Notes:

  • For documents that are not fully accessible and will take us a while to fix.
  • This needs to be sent through email and not the Digital Hub for the links to work.
  • Add a private note to the Digital Hub to say that we’ve sent the email so we can keep a record. Add the details to the accessible documents service contacts spreadsheet.

Email template:

Digital Hub request: INSERT JOB ID

Hello :)

The document that you've asked us to publish on our website doesn't meet accessibility guidelines, so we're currently not able to add this.

This is because: ***Add reason***

It's really important that any document that's published on our website can be read by people who use assistive technology (such as a screen reader). This might be because they have a vision, hearing or cognitive impairment, or motor difficulties.

Take a look at our Digital Hub article for instructions, guides and videos to help you create accessible documents.

Our digital champions also deliver virtual training sessions about how to create accessible documents.

Please reattach the document to the Digital Hub request once it is fully accessible and we'll let you know when this has been published on our website.

Thanks

Digital Team

For documents that we've fixed but need making accessible next time

Notes:

  • For documents that we’ve fixed quickly but service should have made accessible themselves.
  • DO NOT send this if we have fixed something the service can’t fix themselves e.g. a table summary on a PDF. We don’t want to send this to people if a document has taken us a short period of time to fix and they couldn't have actually done it themselves.
  • Add a private note to the Digital Hub to say that we've sent the email. Add the details to the accessible documents service contacts spreadsheet.

Email template:

Digital Hub request: INSERT JOB ID

Hello :)

The document that you've asked us to publish on our website doesn't meet accessibility guidelines.

This was because: ***Add reason***

We've made the document accessible for you on this occasion but can you please make sure any documents that need publishing on our website are fully accessible next time.

It's really important that any document that's published on our website can be read by people who use assistive technology (such as a screen reader). This might be because they have a vision, hearing or cognitive impairment, or motor difficulties.

Take a look at our Digital Hub article for instructions, guides and videos to help you create accessible documents.

Thanks

Digital Team
