Footnotes

Don't use footnotes in your document - these cause confusion for people using screen readers as they can't jump back and forth to read the footnotes. This is an example of footnotes being used:

The use of smartphones has become increasingly prevalent in modern society.1 People rely on these devices for various purposes, such as communication, entertainment, and productivity. However, excessive smartphone use has been linked to negative effects on mental health2 and interpersonal relationships3.

1 Pew Research Center, "Mobile Fact Sheet," last modified September 2021.

2 Elhai, Jon D., et al. "Smartphone Addiction: A Meta-Analytic Review." Journal of Behavioral Addictions 9, no. 2 (2020).

3 Przybylski, Andrew K., and Netta Weinstein. "Digital Screen Time and Mental Well-Being
