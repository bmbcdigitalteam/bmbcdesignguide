How to increase your readability score

You should aim to get your reading score as high as possible. At the very least this should be 60. This is because 60 is classed as 'Plain English', which means that the majority of people will be able to read it.

You should aim to keep your sentences as small as possible, while making sure the sentences still make sense. Aim to keep your sentences under 20 words in length.

You should also try to use simple words in your sentences. Try to keep the majority of your words under three syllables where possible
