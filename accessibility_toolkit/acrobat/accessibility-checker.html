The accessibility checker

The accessibility checker in Adobe Acrobat Pro can be used to check if your PDFs are accessible.

You'll need to make sure all the errors are clear before sending the document through, otherwise we'll have to send them back

This guide should cover most of the issues in Adobe Acrobat Pro. If you have another issue that you can't fix, please email digitalteam@barnsley.gov.uk.
